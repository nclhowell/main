Design Principles

Rule of Thirds

Design Web Kit Dot Com

Rule of thirds is the principle of web layout and design where the most important images are positioned where the eye scans firest, i.e. the upper left 1/3 of the page

Whitespace/Clean Design

Squarespace Dot Com

Whitespace/Clean Design principles follow the "less is more" concept where pages need to be less busy and feature some blank areas in order to divert the focus to the content

Visual Heiarchy

NNgroup Dot Com

Visual Heirarchy rules are followed by the designer who uses tools such as element position, colors, scaling and grouping to guid the visuals of the page towards the most important elements
